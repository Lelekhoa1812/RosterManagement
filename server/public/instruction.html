Manual Instruction

This is the instruction to use our Roster Management System application, you should follow the detailed instruction as below to initialise your business with us:

  1. Sign Up with us:

    Pressing the Log In button on your top right side, and proceed to the Signup page. Enter your credential information as instructed, which will be used as your information preference.

  2. Log In:

    Proceed to Log In page after Sign Up and fill in your username and password as you created previously.

  3. Your Credentials:

    You will see the unique business code generated for you at the home page after login, with the default Admin account information.

  4. Start your app:

    Enter the business code as you starting your Roster Management System app as always.

  5. App level Log In:

    Log in with your admin and staff account (if has been created), the default admin account is given as step 3.

  6. Update your staff and admin account:

    Go to the profile page by clicking to the person icon button at your lower right side to see your account information or update.

  7. App level Sign Up:

    To create a new staff account, click to the 'Create new account' hyperlink and create your own account.

You have reached the end of the Manual Instruction, have fun using our system.
